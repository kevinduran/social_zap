What's a Social Zap board?

Social Tap offers boards and stickers you can place anywhere in your business and allows you to connect with your customers seamlessly.

Social Tap boards are convenient signs that your customers use to access your Social Tap page. Place these signs in strategic locations in your business so your customers can easily connect with you. Every sign is NFC-enabled to make it as simple as a tap. People can also scan the QR code or type in the Tap code into their browser.

After registering for Social Tap, you can purchase a tap board, a tap mini, or stickers. All of these work the same way to get your customers access to your Social Tap page. Tap Boards and Tap Minis can be ordered with an easel stand or a mounting kit. Making it easy to display your Social Tap boards however it fits in your store. Need to reflect your brand? Social Tap offers customization to add your branding to your fully interactive Social Tap stickers and boards.
